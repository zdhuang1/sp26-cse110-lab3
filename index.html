Discussion Notes

Tom stressed that the morning rush is chaotic. The app needs to be incredibly fast so customers don't get grumpy trying to get their coffee.

  • Hardware: They will be using standard 10-inch tablets at the checkout counters.
  • Modifications: The system must handle complex drink modifications (e.g., half-caf, oat milk, exactly 2.5 pumps of sugar-free vanilla).
  • Offline Mode: Crucial for when the shop's Wi-Fi inevitably decides to shutdown.
